Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. It is Portd's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website, portd.io

You are free to refuse our request for your calendar access, with the understanding that we may be unable to provide you with some of your desired services.
Your continued use of our website will be regarded as acceptance of our practices around privacy and personal information. If you have any questions about how we handle user data and personal information, feel free to contact us.
